## What is an AI Hackathon?

An **AI hackathon** (also known as an **artificial intelligence hackathon**) is an intensive, time-limited competition where developers, engineers, and innovators collaborate to build AI-powered solutions using cutting-edge technologies like large language models (LLMs), machine learning algorithms, and generative AI tools. These events can be **online AI hackathons**, **virtual AI hackathons**, **remote AI hackathons**, or **hybrid AI hackathons** that combine both digital and in-person participation.

**How AI hackathons work:** Participants form teams, receive access to AI tools and APIs, and have a limited timeframe (typically 24-72 hours) to develop functional prototypes that solve real-world problems. These events serve as platforms for innovation, learning, and networking within the AI community. **Global AI hackathons** like AI Genesis bring together talent from around the world, making them accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

## Frequently Asked Questions About AI Hackathons

### What is an AI hackathon?

An **AI hackathon** (or **artificial intelligence hackathon**) is a time-limited competition where teams collaborate to build AI-powered solutions using cutting-edge technologies. These events can be **online AI hackathons**, **virtual AI hackathons**, **remote AI hackathons**, or **hybrid AI hackathons** combining both digital and in-person participation.

### How do AI hackathons work?

**How AI hackathons work:** Participants form teams, receive access to AI tools and APIs, and have a limited timeframe (typically 24-72 hours) to develop functional prototypes. Teams present their solutions to judges, and winners receive prizes, recognition, and sometimes job opportunities. The format encourages rapid prototyping, collaboration, and hands-on learning with the latest AI technologies.

### Are AI hackathons free?

Most **AI hackathons** are free to participate in, including registration and access to tools and resources. Some events may charge a small fee for in-person components or premium features, but the majority of **online AI hackathons** and **virtual AI hackathons** are completely free.

### Do AI hackathons pay?

Yes, many **AI hackathons** offer cash prizes, credits, swag, and other rewards to winners. The AI Genesis Hackathon, for example, awarded prizes including $10,000 in GCP Credits, cash prizes, gift cards, and technology products. Additionally, **AI hackathons for jobs** often provide networking opportunities that can lead to job offers and **AI hackathon hiring** connections.

### How long do AI hackathons last?

**AI hackathons** typically last between 24-72 hours, though some may extend to a week. The AI Genesis Hackathon ran for 5 days online (November 14-19, 2025) with a 2-day in-person component. **Online AI hackathons** often have more flexible timelines, while **virtual AI hackathons** may follow stricter schedules.
