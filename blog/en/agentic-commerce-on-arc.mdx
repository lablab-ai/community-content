### The Core Challenge: Autonomous Payments

The mission is to design and ship an application, agent, or developer tool that showcases how **autonomous systems can coordinate, pay, and transact onchain** using Arc and Circle products. The hackathon explores a world where AI agents can pay for APIs, data, compute, and content in dynamic, usage-based ways.

**Arc**, the Economic OS for the internet, provides the Layer-1 settlement with **USDC** as the native gas and value token, offering predictable fees and sub-second finality. This infrastructure enables participants to build truly autonomous systems that can make financial decisions and execute transactions without constant human intervention.

## Technologies and Challenge Tracks

Participants have access to cutting-edge technologies and can choose from multiple challenge tracks to focus their submissions. The hackathon requires building on Arc for settlement and using USDC for all transfers and interactions, ensuring consistency while allowing for creative innovation.

### Required and Recommended Technologies

Participants are required to build on Arc for settlement and use USDC for all transfers and interactions. The recommended technology stack includes:

- **Arc**: The EVM-compatible Layer-1 settlement layer that provides the foundation for autonomous commerce transactions.

- **USDC**: The stablecoin used as the native gas token on Arc, offering stability and predictability for financial transactions.

- **Circle Wallets**: Recommended for primary wallet infrastructure, providing secure and reliable wallet management.

- **Circle Gateway, Bridge Kit, and x402 facilitator**: Additional Circle infrastructure components that enable seamless commerce flows and cross-chain interactions.

These technologies work together to create a comprehensive platform for building autonomous financial systems that can operate independently while maintaining security and reliability.
