Once you have a team, you and your teammates should begin to develop ideas. The most effective way to come up with ideas is to brainstorm as a team. To begin, identify a problem or challenge that you want to solve and then brainstorm potential solutions.

As you decide on a concept, take into consideration the following factors:

* **Is it feasible?** Can the concept be implemented within the time frame of the hackathon?
* **Is it original?** Is the concept unique and different from other ideas being proposed?
* **Is it useful?** Does the concept solve a real problem or need?
* **Is it marketable?** Is there a potential market for the concept?

After deciding on a concept, it's time to start implementing it. This process is likely to involve researching, developing a plan, and executing it. Keep your time management effective and organized.
